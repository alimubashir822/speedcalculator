Frequently AskedQuestions

Find answers to common questions about our calculators, formulas, privacy policy, and features.

What is Speed Calculator?

Speed Calculator is a free web utility suite featuring 40+ highly optimized calculators for speed, distance, travel time, math, finance, academic metrics (GPA and grades), and health/fitness. We aim to replace cluttered, ad-ridden calculation engines with a beautiful, fast, and simple user interface.

Are all these calculators free to use?

Yes, 100% free. There are no signup gates, premium features, daily limits, or hidden fees. You can use any calculator as many times as you like.

Do you save or log my input data?

No. Speed Calculator is designed with strict data privacy. All mathematical computations run directly in your local web browser using client-side JavaScript. None of your entered values are sent to our servers, keeping your records entirely private and secure.

How accurate are the results?

Our calculators are built using industry-standard formulas, official standards (such as the Karvonen formula for target heart rate zones, or US Navy methods for body fat estimation), and physics equations. Results are mathematically precise, though they should be verified for academic or engineering use.

Does the site support both metric and imperial units?

Yes. Calculators that use units (such as distance, weight, or speed) feature simple dropdown menus that allow you to seamlessly switch between metric (km, meters, kg) and imperial (miles, feet, lbs) systems.

Can I suggest a new calculator or feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing user feedback. Please navigate to our Contact Us page or email us at info@speedcalculator.net to request a calculator or suggest improvements.
