At SpeedCalculator.net, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and tools.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from users of our website:

Personal information — such as your name and email address, only when you voluntarily contact us.
Non-personal information — including browser type, device type, operating system, and pages visited, collected automatically via analytics tools.

Note: Our calculators run entirely in your browser. The values you enter (distances, speeds, etc.) are never sent to our servers or stored anywhere.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

To improve our website, tools, and overall user experience
To respond to user inquiries, feedback, and support requests
To analyze website performance and understand how visitors use our tools
Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer. Note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the website.

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services to improve our website:

Google Analytics — to analyze site traffic and understand user behavior. Google may collect anonymized data according to their own privacy policy.
Google AdSense — to display relevant advertisements in the future. These services may use cookies and collect data per their own policies.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services for more information.

Data Protection

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy and the data practices described above. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of the site.
