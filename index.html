Speed Formula

The Speed Distance Time Formula

All calculate speed online tools use one core relationship. Know any two values — get the third instantly with our speed formula calculator.

Find Speed — Velocity Calculator
Speed = Distance ÷ Time

Find Distance — Travel Speed Calculator

Average Speed Calculator — How It Works

The average speed calculator and calculate average speed function works by dividing total distance by total time. Formula: Average Speed = Total Distance ÷ Total Time. If you drive 400 km in 5 hours (including a 30-minute stop), your average speed = 400 ÷ 5 = 80 km/h. Our average speed calculator handles multi-leg trips automatically.

Our running speed calculator and running pace converter converts between pace (min/km or min/mile) and speed (km/h or mph). A 5 km run in 25 minutes = 5 ÷ (25/60) = 12 km/h = 7.46 mph = 5:00/km pace. RPM to Speed & Wheel Speed Calculator

Our rpm to speed calculator and wheel speed calculator converts engine RPM to vehicle speed. Formula: Speed (mph) = (RPM × π × Diameter) ÷ (Gear Ratio × Axle Ratio × 1056). For a bicycle wheel RPM to speed: Speed (km/h) = Wheel RPM × π × Diameter (m) × 60 ÷ 1000. Our wheel speed calculator covers cars, bicycles, motorcycles, and industrial motors.

Typing Speed Calculator — Words Per Minute

Our typing speed calculator measures WPM using the formula: WPM = (Characters ÷ 5) ÷ Minutes. The words per minute calculator also shows accuracy-adjusted WPM. Average: 40-60 WPM. Professional: 70-90 WPM. The typing words per minute standard counts 5 characters as one "word."

Internet Speed & Download Speed Calculator

Our internet speed calculator and download speed calculator formula: Download Time = File Size (MB) × 8 ÷ Speed (Mbps). A 500 MB file at 100 Mbps: (500 × 8) ÷ 100 = 40 seconds. Our internet download speed test calculator converts between Mbps, Kbps, and GB/s for accurate download time estimates.

Speed Calculator FAQ

Everything about speed calculations, unit conversions, formulas, and our free tools.

How do I calculate speed online?

To calculate speed online, use the formula: Speed = Distance ÷ Time. In our speed distance time calculator: select "Find Speed," enter your distance (km, miles, meters) and time (hours, minutes, seconds), then click Calculate. The result appears in your chosen unit — our mph speed calculator, kmh speed calculator, m/s, and knots are all shown simultaneously. Example: 240 km in 3 hours = 80 km/h = 49.7 mph = 22.2 m/s.

What is the average speed calculator formula?

The average speed calculator formula: Average Speed = Total Distance ÷ Total Time. This accounts for the entire journey including stops. Example: driving 300 km with a 1-hour break in 5 total hours = 300 ÷ 5 = 60 km/h average speed. The stop time must be included in "total time." Our calculate average speed tool handles multi-leg trips: add all distances and divide by total elapsed time.

How does the running speed calculator and running pace converter work?

Our running speed calculator and running pace converter converts between pace (min/km or min/mile) and speed (km/h or mph). Enter race distance and finish time to get average running speed. Example: 10 km in 50 minutes = 10 ÷ (50/60) = 12 km/h = 7.5 mph = 5:00/km pace. The pace and speed calculator also projects marathon, half marathon, and 5K finish times at your current pace.

What is the RPM to speed formula and how does the wheel speed calculator work?

The rpm to speed calculator and wheel speed calculator formula: Speed (mph) = (RPM × π × Tire Diameter in inches) ÷ (Gear Ratio × Axle Ratio × 1056). Example: 3000 RPM, 26-inch tire, 4th gear (1.0:1), 3.73 axle = 3000 × 3.14159 × 26 ÷ (1.0 × 3.73 × 1056) = 62.4 mph. Our wheel speed calculator covers cars, motorcycles, bicycles, and industrial motors.

How does the typing speed calculator measure words per minute?

Our typing speed calculator and words per minute calculator formula: WPM = (Total Characters ÷ 5) ÷ Minutes Elapsed. The "standard word" is 5 characters including spaces. Typing words per minute benchmarks: Beginner <30 WPM, Average 40–60 WPM, Professional 70–90 WPM, Speed typist 100+ WPM. Net WPM = Gross WPM minus errors.

How does the download speed calculator work?

Our download speed calculator and internet speed calculator formula: Download Time (seconds) = File Size (MB) × 8 ÷ Speed (Mbps). Example: 4 GB movie at 100 Mbps: (4000 × 8) ÷ 100 = 320 seconds ≈ 5.3 minutes. Key conversion: 1 MB = 8 megabits. Our internet download speed test calculator also converts between Mbps, Kbps, and GB/s for accurate estimates.

What is the playback speed calculator?

Our playback speed calculator and playback rate calculator formula: New Duration = Original Duration ÷ Playback Speed. A 10-hour audiobook at 1.5x: 10 ÷ 1.5 = 6 hours 40 minutes. At 2x: 5 hours. At 0.75x: 13 hours 20 minutes. Works for any video, podcast, or audiobook length and any playback speed from 0.25x to 4x.

How do I convert km/h to mph and other speed units?

Speed unit conversion factors: km/h × 0.6214 = mph. mph × 1.6093 = km/h. m/s × 3.6 = km/h. km/h × 0.5400 = knots. Common conversions: 60 km/h = 37.3 mph, 100 km/h = 62.1 mph, 120 km/h = 74.6 mph. Our speed calculator shows all 5 units simultaneously — mph speed calculator, kmh speed calculator, m/s, knots, and ft/s update automatically.

What is the reading speed calculator?

Our reading speed calculator estimates how long a book or article takes to read. Formula: Reading Time (minutes) = Word Count ÷ Reading Speed (WPM). Average adult reading speed: 200–300 WPM. Example: a 90,000-word novel at 250 WPM = 360 minutes = 6 hours. Enter your word count and reading WPM to get an instant finish time estimate. Also calculates reading time for web articles and documents.

What is the internet speed calculator and top speed calculator?

Our internet speed calculator converts between connection speeds (Mbps, Kbps) and download/upload times for any file size.
