What Is a SIP Calculator?

A SIP calculator (Systematic Investment Plan calculator) estimates how much wealth you will accumulate through regular, periodic investments in mutual funds. You enter three inputs — monthly investment, expected annual return rate, and investment duration — and the SIP calculator formula computes the maturity amount, total wealth gained, and the power of compounding over time. Our India SIP calculator and SIP calculator India are designed for Indian investors using rupee amounts and Indian fund return benchmarks, but it works for any currency or region — including the SIP calculator USA for investors using USD-based index funds or ETFs, where the SIP calculator USA mode simply uses dollar amounts and US market return rates (7–10% for S&P 500 index funds).

SIP Calculator Formula — How It Works

The standard SIP calculator formula for monthly investments is:

M = P × [(1+r)^n − 1] ÷ r × (1+r)

Where: M = Maturity Amount, P = Monthly SIP amount, r = Monthly interest rate (Annual Rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100), n = Total number of months (Years × 12).

SIP Calculator Formula — Worked Example

SIP: ₹5,000/month · Rate: 12% p.a. · Period: 10 years
r = 12 ÷ 12 ÷ 100 = 0.01 · n = 10 × 12 = 120 months
M = 5,000 × [(1.01)^120 − 1] ÷ 0.01 × 1.01
M = 5,000 × [3.3004 − 1] ÷ 0.01 × 1.01
M = 5,000 × 2.3004 ÷ 0.01 × 1.01
M = ₹11,61,695 Maturity Amount
Total Invested = ₹5,000 × 120 = ₹6,00,000
Wealth Gained = ₹11,61,695 − ₹6,00,000 = ₹5,61,695

What Is Step-Up SIP Calculator — Step Up SIP Calculator India

A step-up SIP calculator (also called step up SIP calculator India, top-up SIP calculator, or SIP with step-up) calculates returns when you increase your SIP amount by a fixed percentage every year. As your salary grows, you should grow your SIP — this is the core idea. For example, if you start with ₹5,000/month and increase by 10% every year: Year 1: ₹5,000, Year 2: ₹5,500, Year 3: ₹6,050, Year 10: ₹11,789/month. Our step-up SIP calculator India and SIP calculator with step up mode shows the dramatic difference this makes — often building 2x more wealth than a fixed SIP over 20 years.

Daily SIP Calculator — Is Daily SIP Better Than Monthly?

The daily SIP calculator is for investors who prefer small daily investments (as low as ₹100/day). Mathematically, daily SIP and monthly SIP produce nearly identical results over long durations (the difference is less than 0.1% annually), because the compounding frequency advantage of daily investing is negligible over 10+ years. Monthly SIP is easier to track and manage. However, if your app (like Groww or Paytm Money) offers daily SIP and you prefer the discipline of daily savings, use our daily SIP calculator to see the projected corpus.

SIP planning tip: Start your SIP as early as possible — even ₹1,000/month at age 25 beats ₹3,000/month starting at age 35 (all else equal at 12%). The extra 10 years of compounding on the smaller amount generates more wealth. This is the core insight of our systematic investment plan SIP calculator — time in market beats amount invested.

What is the formula of SIP calculator?

The SIP calculator formula is: M = P × [(1+r)^n − 1] ÷ r × (1+r). Where M = Maturity Amount, P = Monthly SIP, r = Monthly rate (Annual Rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100), n = Total months (Years × 12).
