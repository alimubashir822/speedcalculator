Age Calculator on Date of Birth — Exact Formula

Our age calculator of dob, age calculator on date of birth, and chronological age calculator use this precise algorithm: 1) Calculate complete years between birth date and target date. 2) Subtract years, count remaining complete months. 3) Subtract months, count remaining days. 4) Account for leap years (February 29 birthdays). The formula ensures precision — a person born December 31 at 11:59 PM is only 1 minute old at midnight, not 1 day.

Age Calculator — Worked Example

Born: March 15, 1990 → Calculate age on May 10, 2025
Years: 2025 − 1990 = 35 (birthday March 15 already passed in 2025 ✓)
Months: March 15 → May 10 = 1 full month (April) + partial May
Days: April 15 → May 10 = 25 days
Result: 35 years, 1 month, 25 days (12,835 total days)
Hours: 12,835 × 24 = 308,040 hours · Minutes: 308,040 × 60 = 18,482,400

Dog Age Calculator — Human Years Conversion

Our dog age calculator and age calculator of dog uses the scientifically-accurate method from a 2020 study published in Cell Systems — NOT the outdated "1 dog year = 7 human years" myth. The actual conversion uses a logarithmic formula: Human Age = 16 × ln(dog_age) + 31. Additionally, the conversion varies significantly by breed size — large breeds age faster than small breeds. A 1-year-old dog of any size ≈ 15 human years (adolescence). By age 10, a small dog = ~56 human years while a giant breed = ~78 human years.

Age Calculator on Excel vs Online Age Calculator

The age calculator on excel formula uses: =DATEDIF(DOB,TODAY(),"Y")&" years, "&DATEDIF(DOB,TODAY(),"YM")&" months, "&DATEDIF(DOB,TODAY(),"MD")&" days". While powerful, Excel's DATEDIF function has known bugs with certain date combinations, especially when the day of birth is later in the month than the current day. Our online age calculator handles these edge cases correctly, accounts for leap years precisely, and calculates hours/minutes that Excel doesn't show — making it more accurate and faster for quick age lookups than any spreadsheet formula.

Age Calculator FAQ

Questions covering age calculation from DOB, dog age in human years, UPSC/NDA eligibility, retirement date, baby age in weeks, and chronological vs biological age.

How do I calculate my exact age from date of birth?

Our age calculator of dob / age calculator on date of birth algorithm: 1) Count complete years from birth date to today. 2) Count remaining complete months. 3) Count remaining days. Special cases: If born on Feb 29 (leap year) and current year is not a leap year, birthday is counted on Feb 28 or Mar 1 depending on convention. Example: Born October 20, 1988 → Today May 10, 2025 → 36 years, 6 months, 20 days. Our chronological age calculator also shows this in hours (321,000+) and minutes (19.2M+) for complete precision. Simply enter your DOB in the calculator above and click Calculate Age.

How does the dog age calculator work in human years?

Our dog age calculator and age calculator of dog uses the 2020 Cell Systems scientific model: Year 1 = 15 human years. Year 2 = +9 more = 24 total. Each year after: Small breeds add ~4 human years/year. Medium breeds add ~5/year. Large breeds add ~6/year. Giant breeds add ~7–8/year. Formula: approximately Human Age = 16 × ln(dog_years) + 31. This replaces the outdated "7:1" rule which was never scientifically based. A 5-year-old medium dog ≈ 36 human years (not 35). Use the Dog Age tab in our calculator above — enter your dog's birthdate and select breed size.

How do I check UPSC or NDA age eligibility?

Use our age calculator of upsc and age calculator of nda — click the "On Specific Date" mode. Enter your DOB, then enter August 1 of the exam year (UPSC uses Aug 1 as the reference date). UPSC age limits: General: 21–32 years. OBC: 21–35 years. SC/ST: 21–37 years. PH: +10 years relaxation. For NDA: age must be 16.5–19.5 years on the date of examination. Our age calculator of india mode for exam eligibility shows exactly whether you qualify and how many years/months of eligibility remain. This is our age calculator on specific date mode.

How do I calculate my baby's age in weeks and months?

Our age calculator of baby and age calculator of child mode calculates precisely in weeks and days for newborns and infants. Pediatricians use corrected gestational age for premature babies: Corrected Age = Chronological Age − (40 − weeks gestation at birth) weeks. For a full-term baby born March 1, 2025 → Today (May 10, 2025) = 10 weeks, 2 days old = 2 months, 9 days. The Baby Age mode in our calculator shows both week/day counts and month/day counts, plus percentage of first year completed.

What is the difference between chronological age and biological age?

Chronological age is the precise mathematical age from birth date — our chronological age calculator measures this to the minute. It's the same for every person born on the same date. Biological age measures how old your body functions at a cellular level — it can be 10+ years younger than chronological age with excellent health habits, or older with poor lifestyle choices. Biological age is measured through telomere length, epigenetic markers, and blood biomarkers — it requires medical testing, not a calculator. Our age calculator on today gives your exact chronological age; biological age requires a doctor or specialized health testing kit.

Results show: Your exact retirement date (month, day, year). Years/months/days remaining until retirement. Total working years. Days remaining in current job. This is especially useful for Indian government employees (age 60), PSU employees (58–60), and private sector workers planning early retirement. Our calculator supports retirement ages from 45 (FIRE movement) to 70 (delayed retirement).
