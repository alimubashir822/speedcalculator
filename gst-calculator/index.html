GST Calculator Formula — Add GST and Remove GST

The GST calculator formula has two directions depending on what you know:

Add GST (exclusive → inclusive): GST = Price × Rate ÷ 100 · Final = Price × (1 + Rate/100)

Remove GST / Reverse GST: Pre-Tax = Inclusive ÷ (1 + Rate/100) · GST = Inclusive − Pre-Tax

GST Calculator Formula — Worked Examples

Australia (10%) — Add GST: Product costs $850 (ex-GST). GST = $850 × 10% = $85. Final price = $935.
NZ (15%) — Remove GST: Paid $460 NZD (GST inclusive). Pre-tax = $460 ÷ 1.15 = $400. GST = $60.
India (18%) — Add GST: Service fee ₹5,000. GST = ₹5,000 × 18% = ₹900. Total = ₹5,900.
Canada (5%) — Add GST: Item costs $200 CAD. GST = $200 × 5% = $10. Total = $210.
Singapore (9%) — Remove GST: Receipt shows S$327. Pre-tax = $327 ÷ 1.09 = $300. GST = $27.

Australian GST Calculator — AU GST Guide

Australia's GST is a flat 10% on most goods and services, introduced under the A New Tax System (Goods and Services Tax) Act 1999. The Australian GST calculator and AU GST calculator are the most searched GST tools online. Key facts for our Australia GST calculator:

  • Rate: 10% flat on most goods and services
  • GST-free items: Basic food, health services, education, childcare, financial services, exports
  • Input tax credits: Businesses registered for GST can claim back GST paid on business purchases
  • Registration threshold: Mandatory for businesses with $75,000+ annual turnover
  • Quick formula: To find GST in a price, divide by 11 (e.g., $220 ÷ 11 = $20 GST)

Indian GST Calculator — Multi-Slab System

India's GST system is unique among all major GST countries — it uses five different rates instead of a single flat rate. Our Indian GST calculator includes all five slabs: 0% (essential goods), 5% (basic necessities and economy services), 12% (processed foods, standard goods), 18% (most goods and services — the standard slab), and 28% (luxury goods, tobacco, high-end vehicles). For each item, CGST = SGST = half the total GST rate. Our India GST calculator shows CGST and SGST split automatically.

NZ GST Calculator — New Zealand GST Guide

New Zealand's GST rate is 15%, one of the highest flat GST rates in the world. Our NZ GST calculator and New Zealand GST calculator apply 15% to any amount. Key NZ GST facts: GST was introduced in NZ in 1986 at 10%, raised to 12.5% in 1989, and increased to 15% in 2010. The NZD currency is used. Quick mental math: 15% GST means every $115 inclusive price contains $15 GST (divide the inclusive price by 115 and multiply by 15 to find GST).

Common Questions

GST Calculator FAQ

Questions covering the GST formula, Australia GST, NZ GST, Canada GST, India GST, reverse GST calculator, and how to remove GST from a price.

What is GST and how is it calculated?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a consumption tax levied on goods and services at each stage of the supply chain. The end consumer pays the full GST. Add GST formula: GST Amount = Price × (Rate ÷ 100). Final = Price × (1 + Rate/100). Example at 10%: $500 × 1.10 = $550. GST = $50. Remove GST (reverse): Pre-tax = Inclusive ÷ (1 + Rate/100). Example: $550 ÷ 1.10 = $500. GST = $50. Our easy GST calculator does both instantly — select "Add GST" or "Remove GST" mode.

How do I use the Australian GST calculator?

Click the 🇦🇺 Australia button — the rate auto-sets to 10% and currency to AUD. For Add GST: enter your ex-GST price (e.g. $850). Result: GST = $85, final = $935. For Remove GST (the reverse/remove GST calculator): enter the GST-inclusive price (e.g. $935). Result: GST = $85, pre-tax = $850. The AU GST calculator and Australia GST calculator rate is fixed at 10% by law — you cannot change it for Australian GST calculations.

How does the reverse GST calculator (remove GST) work?

The reverse GST calculator (or remove GST calculator) works backward — you know the GST-inclusive total and want to find the pre-tax price and GST amount. Formula: Pre-Tax Price = Inclusive Price ÷ (1 + GST Rate/100). GST Amount = Inclusive − Pre-Tax. Examples: NZ 15%: $460 ÷ 1.15 = $400 pre-tax, $60 GST. India 18%: ₹2,360 ÷ 1.18 = ₹2,000, GST ₹360. Click the "Remove GST" tab in our online GST calculator and enter the inclusive price — results appear instantly.

What are the GST rates in different countries?

GST rates by country: Australia 10% (flat, most goods/services). New Zealand 15% (our NZ GST calculator / New Zealand GST calculator). Canada 5% federal GST (our Canada GST calculator) — provinces add PST or use HST up to 15%. Singapore 9% (raised from 8% in January 2024). India 0% / 5% / 12% / 18% / 28% — five slabs (our Indian GST calculator lets you choose). Click the country flag button in our GST calculator online to instantly load any country's rate.

How does the Indian GST calculator work with multiple slabs?

India's GST has five slabs: 0% (fresh food, newspapers, basic medicines), 5% (essential goods, transport), 12% (processed foods, smartphones), 18% (most goods, restaurants, IT services — the standard rate), 28% (luxury goods, tobacco, high-end vehicles). Our Indian GST calculator and India GST calculator show CGST + SGST split automatically (each = GST rate ÷ 2). Select the India flag, then choose your slab from the dropdown. For example, a software service at 18%: ₹10,000 + ₹900 CGST + ₹900 SGST = ₹11,800 total.

What is the GST formula for NZ, Canada, and Singapore?

The GST calculator formula is the same regardless of country — only the rate changes. NZ GST calculator (15%): Add: price × 1.15. Remove: price ÷ 1.15. Mental shortcut: GST in inclusive price = inclusive × 3/23. Canada GST calculator (5%): Add: price × 1.05. Remove: price ÷ 1.05. Mental shortcut: GST = inclusive ÷ 21. Singapore GST calculator (9%): Add: price × 1.09. Remove: price ÷ 1.09. Our gst calculator online applies all these formulas automatically — click the country and enter the amount.
